# Grading Rubric

Total: 100

## Design and reasoning: 40

- quality of strategy design: 15
- quality and correctness of pseudocode: 10
- clarity of systems reasoning: 15

## Implementation and experiments: 40

- simulator-based implementation works correctly: 15
- baselines reproduced correctly: 10
- custom strategy comparison is complete: 15

## Analysis and communication: 20

- quality of result interpretation: 10
- discussion of failure modes and limitations: 10

Strong submissions usually show a clear tradeoff argument rather than only chasing the smallest training time.
